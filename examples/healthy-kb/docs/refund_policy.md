title: Refund Policy
---

Refunds for delivered orders require a review step before money is returned. Support agents should confirm the order id, payment status, delivery status, and customer reason. If the order is older than 30 days, the agent must request manager approval before promising a refund. The final answer should cite the support handbook and explain the next action clearly.
